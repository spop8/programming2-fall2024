Stuffed French Toast

Description:

Thick slices of bread, cooked to golden perfection, stuffed with a sweet cinnamon cream cheese filling and topped with tart carmalized apples. Stuffed French Toast is great for a weekend morning breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces of Bread
  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • Cinnamon

Filling

  • 1 Apple
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 4 tbsp Cream Cheese
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Add two eggs, vanilla, sugar, and milk to a bowl and mix.
  2. Heat up pan and add a sprinkling of cinnamon on top of the egg mixture.
  3. Dip your bread into the mixture and then add to the pan.
  4. Let cook for desired time.

Apple Filling

  1. Peel your apple and cut into small chunks.
  2. Add butter to a small pot.
  3. Add in apple, brown sugar, and cinnamon to pot.

Cream Cheese Filling

  1. Add softened cream cheese to a small bowl .
  2. Mix in your sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla.

Assembly

  1. Once french toast and apples are done, it is time to assemble.
  2. First place one piece of french toast and add some of the cream cheese filling, spread. Add apples on top of the cream cheese filling. Top with more apples.
  3. Enjoy!
