Crêpes

Description:

The best crêpes outside of France. These delicate, soft, buttery crêpes will melt in your mouth and leave you wanting more. They are easy to make and scrumptious to taste.

Serving Size: 4-5 people | 🕔 Time: 45 minutes | 🍴Type: Sweet

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 1/2 cups Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 Eggs

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine all dry ingredients.
  2. Next add in all wet ingredients and stir until all lumps are gone.
  3. Heat your crêpe pan
  4. Add 4 tbps/ 1/4 cup of batter to your pan
  5. Spread it around to get nice thin crêpes.
  6. Finish with any fillings you want. I like chocolate and strawberries :)
