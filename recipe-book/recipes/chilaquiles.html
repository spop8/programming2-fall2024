Chilaquiles

Description:

Chilaquiles is a traditional Mexican dish made from lightly fried corn tortillas covered in a flavorful salsa. It is a very versatile dish that can be costumized to your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 Large Eggs
  • 1 Onion
  • 8 Corn Tortillas
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • Chihuahua Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Cut up your tortillas into eighths.
  2. Whisk eggs in a bowl and add salt and pepper.
  3. Cut up your desired amonut of onions and peppers.
  4. Cover your heated pan with oil and add in your onions and peppers.
  5. After a few minutes add in your tortillas. Make sure that each one is coated in oil.
  6. Next, add in your eggs, let it cook for 1 minutes before mixing everything around.
  7. Add salsa on top and mix.
  8. Add cheese on top.
  9. Once the cheese is melted take the pan off the stove.
  10. Optional: Top with avocado :)
  11. Enjoy!
